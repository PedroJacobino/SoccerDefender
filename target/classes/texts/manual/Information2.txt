Information Level
 
Passing your cursor on the Players 
will show their radius and Card 
depending on the Information Level
\n
0: Does not show anything about the 
Slicers or the Towers.
1: Shows the Position of the Tower/
Slicer, its radius and the Tower's 
life
2: Shows the Name and Card with 
the Stats
3: Shows the Shields around the 
Slicers
