Information Showing
 
Shields:
Shields are shown around the players if
they have them, and each shield's width 
depends directly to its current resistance.
\n
SoccerBalls:
SoccerBalls are shown around the Attacker,
he has up to its Power number of Soccer 
Balls, if there are more than 1 soccer 
ball in the same position around the 
player its Sprite will show a different 
Ball to represent that there are 2 or 3 in
the same spot around the player.
